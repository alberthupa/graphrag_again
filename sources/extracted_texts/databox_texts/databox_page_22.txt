Newsletter Subscribers The number of newsletter subscribers can be a valuable KPI for eCommerce businesses. It’s the number of people who are interested in your products or services and have opted to receive regular updates from you. A high email open rate indicates that your email marketing efforts are effective and that your emails are resonating with your audience.
